Weightlifting

Time for a break.

Programming, practicing hobbies, and working professionally in the technology domain means that I familiar with troubleshooting. One method I use to troubleshoot problems while also staying fit and healthy is lifting weights. Taking a break to exercise helps after working on a problem by letting my mind rest and focus on something else before returning to the problem, usually feeling refreshed with new ideas and solutions. Weightlifting is a sport that I started in college after playing baseball from the ages of 5 to 18. It's a sport that has more nuance to it than what I originally thought. Weightlifting, much like programming, has room for growth and experimentation.
